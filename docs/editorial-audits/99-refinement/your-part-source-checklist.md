# Source Checklist

## Source Mode

Source mode: SOURCE_FREE  
External factual claims: none.  
No named sources required.

## Source Gaps

This essay makes no claim about a population, institution, event, study, law, medical condition, or measurable outcome. Its support is ordinary experience and conditional reasoning.

## Claims That Would Trigger A Fresh Source Check

- A statement about how often people experience a bad outcome after careful effort.
- A claim about a named employer, school, program, policy, profession, or public institution.
- A statistic, study finding, medical claim, legal rule, or current event.
- A causal claim that a particular practice produces anxiety, burnout, success, or failure.

## Claims That Must Not Be Made As Certainty

- Careful effort guarantees a desired result.
- A bad result proves a person's character or ability.
- Other people, chance, or timing explain every disappointment.
- Letting go of an outcome means effort no longer matters.
