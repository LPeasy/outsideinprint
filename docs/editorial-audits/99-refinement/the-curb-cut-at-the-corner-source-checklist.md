- Current city transition-plan case: closed with City of Lake Stevens. The public page includes inventory, cost estimate, schedule framework, implementation responsibility, complaint/grievance process, accommodation request form, and technically infeasible ramp process.
- Field example of repaired/upgraded ramps: closed with Minneapolis 50th Street ADA ramp project, which records more than 50 ADA-compliant pedestrian ramps built on a named corridor in summer/fall 2025.
