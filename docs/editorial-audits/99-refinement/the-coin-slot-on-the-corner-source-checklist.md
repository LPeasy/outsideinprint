## Sources Sought But Not Used As Controlling Records

- Broad nostalgic reporting on the last payphone or phone booths. Kept out of the governing structure.
- General smart-city promotional framing. Treated as an interested official/corporate frame.
- Privacy-advocacy criticism of LinkNYC. A useful lens, but not needed to support the essay's central claims.

## Source Hierarchy

1. Patent, statute, FCC rule/order/guidance, public audit, official city record.
2. Direct service terms for the modern replacement platform.
3. Secondary reporting only as background lead material, not as proof.
4. Advocacy or nostalgia frames quarantined and not used as governing authority.
