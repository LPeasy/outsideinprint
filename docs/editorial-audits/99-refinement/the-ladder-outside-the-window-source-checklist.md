## Sources Sought But Not Fully Closed

- Separate original session-law scans for 1862 Chapter 356, 1867 Chapter 908, and 1879 Chapter 504. The public-domain Veiller compilation was used for exact statutory excerpts and source hierarchy.
- A complete citywide condition rate for extant fire escapes. HPD records are violation data, not a full survey.
- A broader city-by-city comparison of outside fire escapes. The essay uses one narrow national context only where it sharpens the New York mechanism.
