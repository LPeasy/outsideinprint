   - HHS-OIG media material, `2026 National Health Care Fraud Takedown`.

3. Prior research:
   - Used only for workflow and OIP source-discipline context.

## Source Gaps

- Final convictions, pleas, dismissals, forfeitures, restitution orders, and recovery totals are not known.
- The package does not verify each of the 455 cases individually.
- The package does not calculate net taxpayer savings.
- The package does not compare this takedown to prior years with an audited denominator.

## Claims That Must Not Be Made As Certainty

- Do not say $6.5 billion was recovered.
- Do not say all charged defendants are guilty.
- Do not say all seized assets will be forfeited.
- Do not say the action produced a specific net savings.
- Do not say the takedown proves motive by any official, party, company, or defendant.

## Currentness Caveats

The main DOJ announcement was published June 23, 2026. This package was created July 1, 2026. Case outcomes can change quickly, so any later publication review should recheck DOJ, HHS-OIG, and relevant court records.
