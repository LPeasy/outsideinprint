While cost reductions have been dramatic, efficiency gains in renewable energy have been more incremental. Such advancements are crucial in maximizing energy yields, particularly in regions with suboptimal conditions.

Drivers of Progress

The progress in renewable energy has been driven by a combination of technological innovation, economies of scale, and supportive policies like subsidies and tax incentives.
