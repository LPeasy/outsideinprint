The tote is the cleanest third product for a first merch release: one size, no breakage risk, and easy packing for manual fulfillment.

## Why a tote

The tote fits the imprint better than a fragile or novelty item. It is practical, durable, and simple to ship without adding operational drag.

## Release notes

This is a one-size launch product. If the tote sells through, the stock note and checkout state on this page will change before new orders are accepted.
